Introduction

The Shadow file converter transforms binary SHADOW files such as BEGIN.DAT to text files preserving their structure. The converter can also convert these text files back into the binary Shadow format.

How to perform conversion

From the drop down menu choose the direction of conversion: from the binary SHADOW files to text or from text files to binary SHADOW files. Press Start button and choose a source and destination files. The conversion process begins and its progress can be observed looking at the progress bar. The process can be interrupted by pressing the Stop button.

Changing conversion parameters

Select Options -> Parameter... and enter the maximum number of rays to process and the first and last columns to write into the text files.
